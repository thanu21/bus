Terms and Conditions

Cancellation of Seats

Passenger can cancel the booked seats with full refund amount if the date of cancellation is before 2 days of the booking through TRT Bus service

Passenger can proceed the cancellation of the seats within 2 days of booking if and only if the bus owner of that seat booked bus accept to do so.TRT Bus service takes no responsibility about refund of payments if bus owner does not accept that cancellation. We do this because of one passenger book the seats in a bus, you are taking the seats of another passenger for that bus.

Even though cancellation is not possible, TRT Bus service will refund whatever the amount agreed by the bus owner to be refunded to the passenger.TRT Bus service will takes no responsible about full refunds.

Normally bus owners accept to cancel the seats if it is sold to some one with in that period. So in that cases,TRT Bus service will refund full amount to the passenger

Seat Booking Rules

TRT Bus service optimize the seat resources and consider about booking with different genders

The system allow any combination of Male seats and Female seats if the booking is done in oneshot

The system will not allow any stranger male booking a seat which is considered as female seat. TRT Bus service takes no responsibility in any cheats in booking which is made as female passenge while it is actually a male passenger

The system will allow a female passenger to book a seat near a male seat.

Boarding in Bus

Passenger are required to board on bus at the correct time which was informed by the bus owner after the booking. TRT Bus service takes no responsibility in missing bus due to the delay by passenger to the boarding place

The bus arrival time may change due to un avoidable reasons. The passenger will be informed about those delays time to time.
